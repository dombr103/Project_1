About Me

I am graduating from the University of Minnesota this spring with a degree in Product Design B.S. and Minor in UX design. My experience in both fields has equipped me with a unique skillset and perspective on creating user-centered solutions. I have a wide range of experience from designing medical devices to consumer goods and mobile applications.

In a recent internship at the Minnesota Children's Museum, I gained real-world experience working with a team to design and fabricate prototypes for the 'Blocklandia' exhibit. I look forward to bringing this experience and passion for design to future opportunities. Please feel free to reach out if you'd like to discuss potential collaborations or just say hello!
